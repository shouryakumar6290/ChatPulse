How to Export Chats

  1. Open the individual or group chat you wish to analyze in WhatsApp.
  2. Tap the three vertical dots (Menu) in the top right corner.
  3. Select More, then tap Export Chat.
  4. Choose Without Media (recommended for faster parsing).
  5. Save the generated .txt file to your device and upload it here.
  1. Open the chat or group inside WhatsApp on your iPhone.
  2. Tap on the **contact name or group subject** at the top of the screen.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom and tap **Export Chat**.
  4. Select **Without Media** in the popup menu.
  5. Select **Save to Files** or share it, then upload the `.txt` here.

ChatPulse operates with 100% in-browser parsing.

Your WhatsApp chats are processed directly inside your browser memory using JavaScript's local FileReader. No files, logs, or chat contents are uploaded to any server, databases, or API endpoints. Your personal conversations remain fully confidential, secure, and private.
